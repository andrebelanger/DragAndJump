How To Play:

  • The goal of the game is to reach the gate!
  • Levels are split into two stages: "Drag" and "Jump."
  • Draw platforms during the Drag stage in order to help you traverse the level during the Jump stage!

Drag Stage controls:

  • Left click and drag to draw a platform.
  • Press enter to end the Drag stage and enter Jump Stage

Jump Stage controls:

  • Arrow keys for movement, and spacebar for jump.
  • Press 'r' to reset the level (also works in Drag Stage).
